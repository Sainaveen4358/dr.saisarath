Advanced Vascular Care

Comprehensive Vascular Treatment Solutions

Our Specialized Services

Peripheral Artery Disease (PAD) Treatment

Management of narrowed or blocked arteries in the legs using medications, angioplasty, or bypass surgery to restore blood flow and alleviate symptoms.

Aneurysm Repair

Treatment of abdominal or thoracic aortic aneurysms with minimally invasive stent grafts or open surgical repair to prevent rupture.

Carotid Artery Surgery

Procedures like carotid endarterectomy or stenting to remove plaque buildup in the carotid arteries and reduce stroke risk.

Varicose Vein Treatment

Minimally invasive procedures, including endovenous laser therapy or sclerotherapy, to relieve discomfort and improve vein appearance.

Deep Vein Thrombosis Management

Treatment of blood clots in deep veins using medications, catheter-based clot removal, or filters to prevent complications like pulmonary embolism.

Venous Insufficiency Management

Addressing chronic venous issues with compression therapy, vein ablation, or other interventions to reduce swelling and improve circulation.

Dialysis Access Creation

Surgical creation of arteriovenous fistulas or grafts to facilitate dialysis for patients with end-stage renal disease.

Arterial Bypass Surgery

Open surgical creation of bypasses to detour around blocked arteries and restore adequate blood flow to limbs.

Endovascular Aneurysm Repair

A minimally invasive procedure to treat aortic aneurysms by placing a stent graft inside the blood vessel.

Thrombolysis and Thrombectomy

Use of medications or catheters to dissolve or remove blood clots and restore blood flow in blocked arteries or veins.

Embolization Therapy

Minimally invasive technique to block abnormal or bleeding blood vessels, commonly used in tumors or arteriovenous malformations.

Vascular Access for Chemotherapy

Placement of ports or catheters to ensure reliable and efficient delivery of chemotherapy or other intravenous treatments.

Management of Vascular Trauma

Emergency surgical or endovascular repair of damaged arteries or veins caused by trauma to prevent life-threatening bleeding.

Lymphedema Treatment

Management of chronic swelling in limbs due to lymphatic blockage through compression therapy, drainage techniques, or surgery.

Thoracic Outlet Syndrome Treatment

Addressing compression of nerves or blood vessels in the upper chest through surgical decompression or other interventions.
